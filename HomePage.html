Malaysia, a vibrant Southeast Asian nation, is known for its diverse culture, stunning landscapes, and rich history.

Malaysia comprises two regions: Peninsular Malaysia and Malaysian Borneo, separated by the South China Sea.

The country boasts a unique blend of Malay, Chinese, Indian, and other ethnics.

The capital city of Malaysia is Kuala Lumpur.
