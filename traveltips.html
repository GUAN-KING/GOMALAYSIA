Travel Tips in Malaysia 😉









Due to the different ethnic groups in Malaysia, there are several useful tips that need to be known before traveling there.


•  Prepare breathable clothing and an umbrella

•  Respect local customs and traditions, especially in public areas.
Alert: Don't eat pork in front of Muslims as a respect

•  Prepare some Malaysian Ringgit (RM) for travel expenses

•  Download online payment application, such as Touch N Go in smartphone

•  Malay (Bahasa Melayu) is the national language in Malaysia. However, English is also widely spoken, especially in tourist areas.

•  Prepare some emergency medical supplies, such as mosquito repellent, fever reducer or others to deal with any situation during the trip.
